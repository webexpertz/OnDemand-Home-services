Scheduling an appointment

Appointments can be scheduled using the downloadable app or by telephone.

Each plan subscription comes with a specified amount of appointments and hours that are included within the subscription price and description.

Silver Plan Subscription includes: 6 appointments, up to 1 hour each per twelve month period beginning on the day you start your subscription (an average cost of 79.98 per hour)

Gold Plan Subscription includes: 6 appointments, up to 2 hours each per twelve month period beginning on the day you start your subscription (an average cost of 69.99 per hour)

Platinum Plan subscription includes: 6 appointments, up to 3 hours each per twelve month period beginning on the day you start your subscription (an average of 66.66 per hour)

Each plan affords 1 scheduled service call per 60 calendar days.

Any appointments that requires longer than the allotted time for your subscribed plan will be billed at the hourly rate of $85.  All billing of time rounds up to the next full hour. Any additional appointments in addition to those included in your plan will also bill at $85 per hour. All billing of time rounds up to the next full hour.
